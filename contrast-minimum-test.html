WCAG 1.4.3 Requirements:

  • Normal Text: Minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio
  • Large Text: Minimum 3:1 contrast ratio
    • Large text = 18pt (24px) or larger
    • Large text = 14pt (18.66px) bold or larger
  • Level: AA
